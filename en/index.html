VISION

K Cube Ventures believes in harnessing
the power of startups to change our world
We believe entrepreneurship the ultimate process of solving real world problems, bettering the quality of all of our lives.
This is why we hold entrepreneurs in the highest regard, and this conviction gives us the reason to stay devoted towards creating world-class nurturing ground for talented people to create startups.

K Cube Ventures specializes in helping entrepreneurs from the early stages.
We have a track record of identifying strong contenders and investing in startup teams even before incorporation.
Our portfolio companies get to join the community network known as the K Cube Family that provide a sense of inclusion, and get to share similar visions and unconditional passion for what they respectively strive for.

HOW WE INVEST

  • FOCUS
    We primarily invest in all things mobile, ranging from mobile services and games, to deep tech.
  • STAGES
    Our focus has been on the seed stage to growth stage startups. We also invest in startups before product launch, service conception or even during team building stage.
  • SCALE
    Our typical investment ranges from $100K to $1.5M per deal.
  • CRITERIA
    We seek for the strongest team with the strongest sense of purpose, camaraderie, and will give all to become the painkillers of our society.

HOW WE VALUE UP

  1. 01 A good discussion partner
    The choice to pursue a startup is not an easy one and startup CEOs face unique challenges every day.
  2. 02 Proactive support
    Startups encounter new inflection points as they grow. Recruiting key developers, bringing C-level executives on board, establishing partnerships with large companies and entry into global markets are among some of these issues.
    K Cube Ventures provides invaluable support in navigating these stages.
  3. 03 Networking
    K Cube Ventures offers networking events such as ‘Family CEO Day,’ a monthly networking event for our family companies. It is a forum for executives to share advice and discuss opportunities for cooperation with one another.
  4. 04 Knowledge sharing
    K Cube family companies have access to an innovative online and mobile platform where they can share their best practices and past experiences.
    This knowledge sharing system helps companies to make more efficient and effective executive decisions.
  5. 05 Business support
    Our experts provide startups with practical assistance in handling legal, tax & accounting, PR/marketing and human resources issues.
